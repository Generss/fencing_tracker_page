Bluetooth Fencing Tracker

Project Overview

A budget wireless fencing tracker

The purpose of this project was to make a device that can detect and track fencing hits while not erroneously tracking misses on the bellguard as hits, all while being wireless and within a cost much lower than similar products on the market. As well as being more cost effective, this system has some bonus features some added features (such as AWS updates and manual point update capabilities).

Details

What this part shows

The video demonstrates how our device can determine hits, while not counting hits that are invalid such as if the tip hits the bellguard of the opponents weapons

After the detection is made a bluetooth signal is sent to a central arbitration device (the CC3200) over bluetooth. This will only occur on a valid hit.

Highlight 1: Detects valid hits
Highlight 2: Won't register invalid hits
Highlight 3: Sends data regarding the hits over bluetooth to arbitration device.
